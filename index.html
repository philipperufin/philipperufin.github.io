Philippe Rufin
F.R.S. FNRS Postdoctoral Fellow, Université Catholique de Louvain
philippe.rufin@uclouvain.be

I am a Geographer working at the intersection of Earth Observation and Land System Science. I am currently working at the Earth and Life Institute at UCLouvain, supported by a F.R.S.-FNRS Postdoctoral Fellowship. My studies make use of Earth Observation data and Machine Learning techniques to map land use, land management, and land intensity across large regions and long time frames. My recent work focuses on croplands and pastures in consolidated and small-holder systems within tropical and semi-arid regions.
